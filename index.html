STORM REID

Storm Reid is an American actress. After playing a cameo role in the television film A Cross to Bear (2012), Reid made her acting debut with an appearance in the period drama film 12 Years a Slave (2013).

ZENDAYA COLEMAN

Zendaya Maree Stoermer Coleman (/zənˈdeɪ.ə/ zuhn-DAY-uh; born September 1, 1996) is an American actress and singer. She has received various accolades, including a Primetime Emmy Award. Time magazine named her one of the 100 most influential people in the world on its annual list in 2022. Oakland, California, U.S.

MARSAI MARTIN

Caila Marsai Martin is an American actress, best known for her role as Diane Johnson on the ABC sitcom Black-ish (2014–2022). Plano, Texas, U.S. In 2019, Martin starred in the comedy film Little, which she also produced, becoming the youngest person ever to produce a studio film. Born: Caila Marsai Martin; Plano, Texas, U.S
